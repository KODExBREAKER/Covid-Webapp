Social Distancing Guidelines

  • Maintain at least 1 meter (3 feet) distance from others.
  • Avoid crowded places and gatherings.
  • Wear a mask in public settings where social distancing measures are difficult to maintain.
  • Cover your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when coughing or sneezing.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell or have any symptoms.
  • Follow local health authority guidelines and recommendations.
